Welcome to the LPFHRA Media Upload Guide

Welcome! This page will guide you on how to upload a video or audio file to get a link you can use to post on your LPFHRA page.

You cannot upload large video or audio files directly on the platform. To make this easy for all LPHRA members, we have provided a simple solution.

How to Upload and Get a Link

It's very simple. Just follow these steps:

1

Click the button below to open **Catbox.moe**. This website allows you to upload media files and get a direct link for free.

Open Catbox.moe
2

On the Catbox.moe page, click the **"Choose File"** button to select your audio or video file from your device. After selecting, the upload will start automatically.

3

Once the upload is complete, you will see a **"File URL"** link. Copy this link. This is the link you will use for your post on the LPHRA platform.

How to Post with Your Link

After you have copied your media link, you can use it to create a post on LPFHRA. This is what you do:

1

In the post box on your LPHRA page, paste the audio or video link you just copied. The system will automatically recognize the link.

Your post will look like this:

You can also use a link from other platforms like **YouTube** for videos, or any direct link for audio files. Just make sure it's a valid link.

If you have questions or need more help, feel free to contact us via email or ask a nearby LPHRA member for assistance.

Yadda Zaka Loda kuma ka Samu Link

Yana da matukar sauki. Bi wadannan matakai:

1

Danna maɓallin da ke ƙasa don buɗe **Catbox.moe**. Wannan shafin zai ba ka damar loda fayilolin bidiyo da audio kyauta kuma ka samu link ɗinsu kai tsaye.

Buɗe Catbox.moe
2

A shafin Catbox.moe, danna maɓallin **"Choose File"** (Zaɓi Fayil) don zaɓar bidiyo ko audio daga na'urarka. Bayan ka zaɓa, lodawar za ta fara kai tsaye.
