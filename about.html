About Leadway Peace Functions and Human Rights Aids (LPFHRA)

Leadway Peace Functions and Human Rights Aids (LPFHRA) is a non-governmental organization established to serve humanity. Our main objective is to promote peace, ensure justice, and protect human rights in every corner of the world. We were not founded for any single country or region but for all communities globally.

We work diligently to ensure that every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity, without facing oppression, discrimination, or injustice. We believe that peace and justice are not privileges for the strong, but a right for every human being.

Our Objectives and Programs

LPFHRA's programs are extensive and cover various areas to achieve our goals. We work hard to:

Promote Peace and Unity:

We conduct training and awareness workshops in cities and villages to teach peaceful conflict resolution methods. We bring together community leaders, elders, youth, and women to create strategies that ensure peace. For example, we can organize a meeting where there is a conflict between two ethnic groups and invite their leaders to sit at a single table to discuss and find a solution without using force.

Protect Human Rights and Prevent Oppression:

We fight against all forms of oppression, abuse, and discrimination. We focus particularly on protecting the rights of children, women, and other vulnerable people. For example, if we receive a report that a girl has been abused, our organization will intervene with lawyers to ensure she receives justice and that the perpetrator is punished. Similarly, we fight against any form of child labor and support children in returning to school.

Improve Education and Awareness:

We conduct workshops and awareness campaigns on radio, television, and social media to educate the public about their rights and the importance of peace. We provide important information on the rights of women and children, and how to build good upbringing within the family. We have expert teachers and consultants who provide knowledge on modern strategies to improve the lives of the community.

Ensure Justice and Adherence to Law:

We work with legal officials, such as lawyers, judges, and security agents to ensure law and order are followed. We monitor any case of injustice and contribute to the courts by providing truthful evidence. If a poor person says they have been abused and cannot afford a lawyer, LPFHRA will advise and support them to get justice.

Our Workflow and Modern Technology

LPFHRA uses modern technologies to achieve our goals quickly and efficiently.

Modern Communication Channels:

We use websites, social media, and mobile apps to spread our message, educate people, and receive reports. Through our online radio station, we broadcast our programs to communities worldwide.

Analysis and Research:

We have expert researchers who analyze community problems and research how to bring about change. We conduct research on human behavior and the strategies to address them to provide effective working methods.

Reporting System:

We have a special system on our website where anyone can report or file a complaint about oppression or abuse they have faced or witnessed. Evidence can be sent to us via video, audio, or photo. This system gives everyone the opportunity to be a monitor and contributor to justice.

How to Join Us

LPFHRA welcomes all honest, dedicated, and contributing people to our community.

How to Become a Member:

To become a member of LPFHRA, or to establish a branch in your country or town, the first step is to contact the LPFHRA leaders near you. After that, you must complete the LPFHRA online Course. This course will teach you the organization's mission, peace strategies, and how to become a true representative. We need dedicated people who will work hard and be self-reliant.

Partnerships:

We work with any organization or individual who desires community development. This includes lawyer associations, human rights organizations, business groups, business people, leaders, students, employees, and even ordinary citizens. Everyone can contribute to building a just world.

Reporting and Complaint System:

We have a system where anyone can file a complaint or report. If you see someone being oppressed, you can fill out a form on our website with a photo, video, or audio. LPFHRA members near you will receive this complaint and help until justice is served.

Our Special Principles

LPFHRA does not tolerate abuse, discrimination, or differences based on region, religion, or ethnicity. We respect all people equally, and we adhere to national and international laws. We believe that every community is one, and everyone has the right to justice regardless of any differences.

Special Group

Only LPFHRA members who have been granted permission and a work certificate are authorized to disseminate information and reports on the organization's activities. This ensures that every message disseminated by us is reliable and authentic, as everyone knows that the information comes from the source.

We believe that through partnership and hard work, we can build a peaceful society where everyone can be proud to be a citizen with dignity and rights.
